2.  Describe the purpose of a DIV tag.

A DIV tag is a way to isolate a specific element, or series of similar elements, in HTML so that you can manipulate them in CSS. It is also helpful with organizing your HTML and CSS files because you can name your tag to correspond with the content the tag is for.

10.  Why do we use media queries?

We use media queries so that our page can be responsive when it is opened on different devices with different-sized pages. Using media queries, the pages and their elements, including pictures and graphics, can fit onto the width of different-sized screens.


11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

The biggest difference between the two types is that serif fonts have serifs, or small foots or features at the end of letters, while sans-serif fonts do not.
